High Elves

Elves are immortal, living a life without an end. These delicate and gracefull being are the elders of this world. Elves have close ties to nature and life. They love music, lore and dance, but are solitary beings, preferring to seek their pleasures far from the prying eyes of the world. When intruders come to their forests they seldom escape, and those who are taked from an Elven forest, after having dwelt among elves, often die of broken hearts and remorse.
